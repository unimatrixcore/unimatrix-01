BerryBot started as a response to monetization-heavy server bots. The goal is to cover the moderation and administration basics communities actually need without turning core server operations into upsells.

Transparency is part of the product, not just the README. The project is meant to stay inspectable, forkable, and adaptable so communities can understand how it works, make their own changes, and avoid getting trapped behind a closed platform.

The current direction stays grounded in practical server management: self-roles, onboarding, moderation workflows, and the kinds of operational features that help staff teams stay consistent without overcomplicating the bot.
